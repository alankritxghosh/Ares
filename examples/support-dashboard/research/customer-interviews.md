# Customer Interviews

## Support Manager Notes

- Customers repeatedly complain about slow response time during high-priority incidents.
- Customers say repeated handoffs make them feel like they are explaining the same issue again.
- Support managers want visibility into overloaded agents before service levels slip.
- Managers need a simple way to spot unresolved escalations by priority and customer region.

## Interview Signals

- A daily queue view would help managers rebalance work before the end of the day.
- Weekly reporting should show response time, satisfaction score, open tickets, and escalation trends.
- The first dashboard version should focus on team visibility before adding automation.
